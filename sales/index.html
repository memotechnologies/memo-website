How it works

From notes and meetings to action.

Notes in. Everything else out. Memo updates the CRM the way a human would, drafts the email that should follow, and books the meeting that’s next.

Context arrives.

From a Fireflies or Gong transcript, a calendar event with notes attached, or a DM you sent yourself in plain English. No copy-paste, no forms.

Memo reasons.

The agent searches the CRM, deduplicates contacts and companies, validates deal-stage fields, and surfaces the overdue task that’s already on file.

You review & upload.

The proposed updates, follow-up email, and next-step invite arrive in plain English. Tick what you want, untick what you don’t. One tap to send.
